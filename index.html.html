SMUD Rebates

What you qualify for

SMUD increased rebate levels in February 2026 after federal tax credits expired. These amounts are stackable for qualifying projects.

$3,000
Gas-to-Electric Upgrade
Replacing a gas HVAC system with a qualifying electric heat pump. Highest rebate tier. Most common upgrade in Sacramento's aging housing stock.
Residential & multifamily eligible
$1,000
Electric-to-Electric Upgrade
Replacing an existing electric system with a newer, high-efficiency heat pump. Qualifies for variable-speed and two-stage systems at or above 15 SEER2.
Stackable with water heater rebate
$4,000
Heat Pump Water Heater
Install a qualifying heat pump water heater alongside your HVAC upgrade. Stackable with either HVAC rebate for a combined total up to $7,000.
Combined maximum: $7,000
